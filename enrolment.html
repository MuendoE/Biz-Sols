How to Enrol

Join our GCP training and CRA simulations in four simple steps. Create your account, request your place, pay by EFT or online card, and start learning.

Enrolment in Four Steps

Getting started is simple. We'll enrol you as soon as your payment is confirmed.

1

Create your account

Register free on our learning portal at learn.alphacrsolutions.co.za and confirm your email.

2

Request enrolment

Email enrolments@alphacrsolutions.co.za with the course you'd like to join. We'll confirm the fee and payment options.

3

Make payment

Pay the course fee by EFT or securely online by card via PayFast — whichever suits you.

4

Get enrolled & start

Once your payment reflects, we enrol you in the course — log in and start learning right away.
