Enrolment in Four Steps

Getting started is simple. We'll enrol you as soon as your payment is confirmed.

1

Create your account

Register free on our learning portal at learn.alphacrsolutions.co.za and confirm your email.

2

Request enrolment

Email enrolments@alphacrsolutions.co.za with the course you'd like to join. We'll confirm the fee and payment options.

3

Make payment

Get enrolled & start

Enrolment FAQs

Common questions about enrolling in our GCP training and CRA simulations in South Africa.

How do I enrol in an Alpha CR Solutions course?

Create a free account on our learning portal, email enrolments@alphacrsolutions.co.za to request your course, pay the fee by EFT or online card, and we enrol you as soon as your payment reflects.

How much do the GCP training and CRA simulation courses cost?

Course fees vary by programme. Email enrolments@alphacrsolutions.co.za with the course you are interested in and we will send current pricing and payment details.

What payment methods do you accept?

You can pay by EFT / bank transfer or by debit or credit card online via PayFast, South Africa’s trusted payment gateway.

Do I receive a certificate?

Yes. On completing the course and its assessment you receive an Alpha CR Solutions certificate of completion, evidencing the training you have undertaken.

Is the GCP training aligned with ICH GCP?

Yes — our Good Clinical Practice training is aligned with ICH E6(R3) GCP and contextualised for South African investigative sites and SAHPRA requirements.

Who are the courses for?

Our training suits Clinical Research Associates (CRAs), investigators, study coordinators and site staff — from new entrants to experienced clinical research professionals looking to build or prove their competency.

Create your account on our learning portal, then request enrolment in your chosen course.
