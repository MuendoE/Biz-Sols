About

Experience you can build a study on.

Alpha CR Solutions is led by Godfrey Eric Muendo, a senior clinical research associate based in Johannesburg with more than six years of experience across the clinical trial lifecycle. The work has spanned hands-on site monitoring, clinical systems, and quality, with sponsors and contract research organisations including Diagnosearch Life Sciences, ICON, Labcorp, and Sanofi.

Godfrey monitors trials both on-site and remotely, in blinded and unblinded capacities, across South Africa and West Africa. His experience covers a broad range of therapeutic areas, among them oncology, immunology, rheumatology, nephrology, cardiology, gastroenterology, and infectious diseases and vaccines, including malaria and HPV studies as well as work with a paediatric component.

Background & qualifications

  • Bachelor of Engineering, Chemical Engineering, University of Pretoria
  • Master of Engineering Management, University of Pretoria, in progress
  • SAHPRA Good Clinical Practice certified
  • Candidate Chemical Engineer, Engineering Council of South Africa (ECSA)
  • Extensive experience with EDC, IRT, eTMF, and CTMS platforms

Approach

Every engagement is grounded in one principle, which is to protect the integrity of the data and the safety of the people the research is meant to serve. Godfrey applies a systematic SDR-before-SDV methodology that reduces downstream data cleaning and supports cleaner database locks, and holds himself to a standard of first-cycle approved monitoring visit reports. An engineering and systems background brings a process-improvement mindset to clinical operations, including the design of new clinical trial tools and architecture.

Who we work with

We support sponsors, contract research organisations, investigator sites, and research teams who need experienced, detail-driven clinical research expertise, whether for a single study or ongoing partnership.
