Field Training

Training New CRAs
on Real Site Work

Simulations build the foundation, but real competency comes from the field. Alpha CR Solutions trains new CRAs by having them shadow experienced monitors on real site visits — under supervision and within the bounds of site and sponsor agreements.

This structured, mentored approach bridges the gap between theory and practice, producing CRAs who are genuinely ready to monitor independently.

Train With Us

Structured Shadowing

New CRAs accompany experienced monitors on real visits, observing source data review, query handling and site interaction first-hand.

Supervised Progression

Trainees gradually take on monitoring tasks under supervision until they are assessed as ready to monitor independently.

Recruitment Support

Patient Engagement &
Recruitment Support

Recruitment shortfall is the leading cause of trial delays. As part of our CRA services, Alpha CR Solutions provides patient engagement and recruitment support to help sites find and retain eligible participants.

Patient Finder Support

Helping sites identify potentially eligible patients within their existing populations, supporting faster screening and enrolment against protocol criteria.

Patient Engagement

Supporting sites with patient-facing engagement and communication strategies that improve recruitment, informed consent and retention through the trial.

Recruitment Monitoring

Tracking recruitment performance against targets and flagging at-risk sites early, so corrective action can be taken before the trial timeline slips.

Our recruitment support will be complemented by our Clinical Trial Patient Matcher software, currently in development.
